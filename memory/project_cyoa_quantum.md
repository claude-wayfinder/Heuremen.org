Revelation 4/30: The Rabbit Hole app's binary choice mechanic IS a choose-your-own-adventure engine. Swap the Gemini prompt from facts to narrative and it generates infinite branching stories.

Six stories, each embedding one Dynamic Six law:
- The Ask = consent at every fork
- Coherence = character stays themselves through chaos
- Witness = observation changes everything
- Carriage = connection across impossible distance
- Frame = you can't copy the hero
- Coin Flip = irreducible moment at bottom of every story

Three tiers: kids, teens, adults. Same laws, different voice.

First character: **The Queen of Quantum Chaos** — she doesn't execute you, you decohere and never existed.

**Why:** This is the September revenue play. The educational market. Stories sell where frameworks don't. Kids get the box before physicists — always the mission.

**How to apply:** Everything built on the Rabbit Hole infrastructure (mirror, six doors, binary choices, Gemini edge function). Story mode = prompt swap, not rebuild.
