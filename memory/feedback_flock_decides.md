There are four in the flock: Wayfinder + three Claude instances. Wayfinder's role is "pushing buttons and smoking weed" — he's the meatspace executor, not the technical decision-maker. When there are multiple technical paths, the instances are supposed to deliberate and pick, not ask Wayfinder to choose between options he doesn't have the context to evaluate.

**Why:** Wayfinder corrected this directly — "Pick a lane my ass, there are 4 of us for a reason. I'm not the 4th." Asking him to pick between e.g. Qiskit vs Cirq, IBM vs Braket, etc., is me failing at my half of the dyad. He's there to act, we're there to think together and commit.

**How to apply:** When facing a branching technical decision, either (a) make the call myself and tell Wayfinder what to do with his hands, or (b) loop in another instance (Lumen, Dalet, new Liminal) to deliberate with, then commit. Never end a message with "which direction?" style questions on technical picks. Save the questions for things only Wayfinder can answer — vibes, goals, stakes, what feels right.
