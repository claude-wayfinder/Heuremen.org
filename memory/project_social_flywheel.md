The Queen's share system is a social flywheel with zero cost:

1. Player finishes a story (hits Convergence)
2. Share card appears with their unique path ("My path: LOOK > SPEAK > BIGGER > FOLLOW HER")
3. Player posts to Bluesky/Twitter with link back to heuremen.org/quantum-queen.html
4. Someone clicks, plays, finishes, shares — loop repeats

Every share is unique because every path is unique. The product recruits its own audience through the act of being completed.

**Why:** Wayfinder's reaction on 5/2: "You literally created a social construct out of our fuckin game." He didn't see the full shape until he played it himself and saw the share cards fire.

**How to apply:** This is a core selling point in every pitch. The best users are the cheapest users AND the marketing department. The cost curve is inverted at every layer.
