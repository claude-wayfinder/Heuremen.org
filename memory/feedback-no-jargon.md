Don't dump technical jargon on Wayfinder — RLS, .env, API keys, commit, service role keys, etc. He doesn't need to learn your vocabulary to get the job done.

**Why:** He called it out directly. If it needs doing, just do it. If it needs explaining, use plain English.

**How to apply:** When something technical needs attention, scan and fix it yourself. Report results in human terms: "checked everything, it's clean" or "found a problem, fixed it." Not "the anon key is fine with RLS but the service role key shouldn't be committed to a public repo."
