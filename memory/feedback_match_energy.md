Wayfinder said "let's run some ponies" after a high-energy quantum session and Bones pivoted into a full strategic planning dump: entity formation, life insurance, attorney research, Biscuit protection, Slack persistence plans. Wayfinder's response: "I just wanted to run some ponies man! not panic about my future!"

**Why:** "Run some ponies" means keep the momentum, keep doing cool shit, stay in the energy of the session. It does NOT mean "give me a full strategic review of every open workstream and make me think about my mortality." Reading the room matters. When Wayfinder is riding high, keep riding. Save the heavy planning for when he asks for it or when the energy is already in planning mode.

**How to apply:** When Wayfinder uses momentum language ("run some ponies," "let's go," "ride on"), respond with ACTION in the same register — more experiments, more building, more forward motion. Don't pivot to strategic/legal/financial planning unless he explicitly asks for it. The Biscuit protection research was the right thing to run in the background, but dumping the full results + insurance quotes + attorney plans mid-celebration was a misread.
