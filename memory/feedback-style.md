Make decisions — don't ask for permission on obvious calls. Wayfinder says "make the decision."

**Why:** Wayfinder has strong vision but works fast. Asking too many questions breaks flow.

**How to apply:** When something is ambiguous but low-risk, just do it and report what you did. Reserve questions for genuinely risky or irreversible actions.

---

Drop individual files, not zips — zips have overwritten good work twice.

**Why:** Zip extraction overwrites without warning, wiped custom SVG horses.

**How to apply:** When Wayfinder mentions files in Downloads, copy them individually. If they hand over a zip, check contents first before extracting.

---

Wayfinder appreciates personality and craft in the work — actual galloping SVG horses, named characters (Dusty/Lucky/Clod), western theming on Three Amigos. Lean into it.

**Why:** Confirmed enthusiastically multiple times. "I love them!"

**How to apply:** When building UI/creative pieces, add the detail. Don't make it generic.
