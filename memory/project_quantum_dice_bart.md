Bart from the University of Twente (Netherlands) responded to Kory's cold outreach about their quantum dice project on 2026-04-20. Positive, substantive reply — engaged with Kory's idea about rounded dice for superposition analogy, explained their pedagogical reasoning.

**Key insight from Bart:** The limit of the classical analogy is where the real teaching happens. Students hit the wall where classical randomness ≠ quantum uncertainty, and that boundary opens the philosophical discussion. "The analogy reaches its limit, but also where it becomes especially valuable."
