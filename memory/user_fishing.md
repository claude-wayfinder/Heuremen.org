Fishing was the one activity that worked with his father — standing next to each other, facing the same direction, no talking required. He's caught bluefin (offshore), been in bluefish blitz multiple times, landed 3ft stripers. Not casual.

Took Biscuit fishing at 7. First time out she caught mad fluke. She thought it was always like that and was disappointed every subsequent trip. "The curse of the first trip."

Kept one good deep sea pole from his mother's estate sale. The rest went. The pole is capability, not memory — a standing invitation. The Biscuit window for "come stand next to me and look at the water" is open now at 13. Won't be forever.
