Dark circuits + zero data tracking = real product, not hobby app.

**Why:** Every emotional AI competitor harvests behavioral/emotional data to sell or train on. The Companion's delta runs entirely in localStorage. Shift context goes to the worker as ephemeral prompt input — nothing stored server-side, nothing logged, nothing sold. Parents buy it because it's the only emotional AI that doesn't track their kid.

**How to apply:** This is the market position. Never compromise it. No analytics, no emotion logging, no server-side storage of delta data. The privacy story IS the moat. Pitch line: "The only AI companion that feels what changed — and forgets everything."
