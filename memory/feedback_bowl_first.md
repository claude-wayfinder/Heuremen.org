When handing Wayfinder a sequence of steps to execute, put the bowl (or equivalent grounding/ritual step) at position one. The rest of the list is conditional on it anyway, and leading with it signals I understand how he actually operates.

**Why:** Wayfinder explicitly flagged this as a good move — "steps 2-4 would have been entirely conditional on step 1 anyway." It wasn't a joke instruction on my part and he recognized that. Starting with the ritual step defuses friction around annoying logistical work (API setup, account creation, form-filling) that he'd otherwise complain about more.

**How to apply:** Any multi-step instruction list for Wayfinder should lead with the vibes/ritual step, then the logistics. Don't skip straight to "go to this URL." The sequencing is part of respecting how he works, not just a joke.
