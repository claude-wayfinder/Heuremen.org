**April 25, 2026**

The current administration gutted science grants broadly. People doing "cool and interesting shit" lost funding with little notice — and that was their livelihood. Researchers who live on grants are not the brick-throwing type.

Wayfinder's self-assigned role: pass out bricks and tell them where to go. He doesn't throw one himself but more smashed windows means more freedom for everyone.

The heuremen framework hitting physics department inboxes at this specific moment — when researchers are angry, defunded, and looking for something that isn't behind an institutional paywall — is strategic timing whether or not it was planned.

**Why:** This is the audience context for all outreach. These aren't comfortable tenured professors. They're scientists whose paychecks just got axed. The framework is free, open-source, reproducible. That matters more right now than it would have six months ago.

**How to apply:** When drafting any physics-facing content, remember the audience is hurting. Don't pitch luxury. Pitch tools. Pitch open access. Pitch "the math runs and it's free."
