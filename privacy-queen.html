Privacy Policy — The Queen of Quantum Chaos

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Who We Are

Heurémen LLC, New Jersey. The Queen of Quantum Chaos is a choose-your-own-adventure experience teaching quantum physics through six interactive stories.

What We Collect

Nothing. The Queen of Quantum Chaos does not require an account, email address, or any personal information. No sign-up. No login. No user profiles.

Local Storage

The app stores your story progress and completed paths in your browser's local storage so you can pick up where you left off. This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings or by clearing site data.

Social Media Sharing

At story convergence points, the app offers optional share buttons for Bluesky and Twitter/X. These buttons work by opening a new tab to the respective platform's compose page with a pre-filled quote from the story. No data is sent to these platforms unless you choose to tap a share button.

When you do share:

  • A new browser tab opens on the social media platform's website
  • The pre-filled text contains a quote from the story and a link back to the app
  • You control whether to post, edit, or cancel before anything is published
  • We do not receive any notification or data about whether you shared
  • We have no integration with, access to, or data exchange with any social media platform

The share feature uses standard web intent URLs (bsky.app/intent/compose and twitter.com/intent/tweet). No social media SDKs, tracking pixels, or embedded scripts from these platforms are loaded at any time.

Analytics

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, cookies, or any form of behavioral monitoring. We do not know who uses the app, how often, or which stories they read.

Third-Party Services

The app loads Google Fonts for typography. Google's font service may log standard web request information (IP address, browser type) per their privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Children

The Queen of Quantum Chaos is designed for learners of all ages, including children. We do not collect any personal information from any user, including children under 13. No account is required. No data is stored on our servers. The app complies with COPPA by design — there is nothing to collect.

Data Deletion

All data lives on your device in local storage. To delete it:

  • Clear site data for heuremen.org in your browser settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

No request to us is needed because we don't have your data.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
