Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

What The Dyad Is

The Dyad is a conversational AI service operated by Heurémen LLC, New Jersey. You bring a problem, work it with an AI mind, and the finding lives in the space between you.

What We Collect

When you use The Dyad, your messages are sent to our server to generate responses. We do not require an account, email address, or any personal information to use the service.

Conversation Data

Your conversations are processed in real time to generate responses. We do not store conversation content on our servers after the session ends. We do not train AI models on your conversations.

Analytics

We use basic, anonymous analytics (page views, session counts) to understand how the service is used. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

The Dyad uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that processing. We use Cloudflare for hosting and delivery.

Cookies

The Dyad does not use cookies or tracking pixels. The service worker caches static assets locally for performance only.

Children

The Dyad is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please use the service with a parent or guardian present.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
