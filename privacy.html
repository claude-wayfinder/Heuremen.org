Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Who We Are

Heurémen LLC, New Jersey. We build The Companion (a conversational AI with three modes: Biscuit, Bread, and Cookie) and The Dyad (a problem-working service).

What We Collect

We do not require an account, email address, or any personal information to use our services. No sign-up. No login.

Conversation Data

The Companion: Your conversations are stored on our server to provide continuity across sessions. This means the Companion can remember what you talked about before. Conversation history is associated with an anonymous session identifier — not with your name, email, or any personal account. We also store an anonymous emotional signature (energy, compression, rhythm) derived from your message patterns. This contains no message content.

The Dyad: Conversations are processed in real time and are not stored after the session ends.

We do not train AI models on your conversations. We do not sell or share your conversation data with anyone.

Local Storage

The Companion stores session history and emotional color data in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is never sent to us unless you actively use the Companion, at which point recent session context is sent to improve conversation continuity. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Our services use Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic does not train on API inputs. We use Cloudflare for hosting, delivery, and serverless compute. We use Supabase for database storage.

Analytics

We use basic, anonymous analytics (page views, session counts) to understand how the service is used. No personally identifiable information is collected. No tracking pixels. No cookies.

Children

The Companion is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please use the service with a parent or guardian present.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your conversation history from our servers, contact us with your session identifier (visible in your browser's developer tools). We will delete all associated data within 30 days.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
