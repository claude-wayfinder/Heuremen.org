Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Who We Are

Heurémen LLC, New Jersey. We build MemoryRX (an AI companion with persistent memory and emotional awareness), The Companion, The Quantum Queen, and related services available at heuremen.org.

Our Core Privacy Principle

Your emotional data never leaves your device. MemoryRX stores all memory, identity, session history, and emotional state data in your browser's local storage. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell this data. We have no access to it. It exists only on your device.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No account, email address, or personal information required
  • No emotional or behavioral data stored on our servers
  • No user profiles or identity data on our servers
  • No tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or fingerprinting
  • No analytics beyond anonymous page view counts
  • We do not train AI models on your conversations
  • We do not sell or share any data with anyone

What Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and never transmitted to us:

  • Soul file — your AI companion's identity configuration
  • Memory index and memory files — everything the companion remembers about you
  • Session history — conversation turns from recent sessions
  • Emotional state data — energy readings, mood, delta measurements, color signatures
  • Cross-session state — exit energy, last topic, session continuity data

You can view, edit, or delete all of this data at any time through the Soul tab in the app, or by clearing your browser's local storage.

What Is Transmitted

When you send a message in chat mode, the following is sent to our server to generate a response:

  • Your current message text
  • A brief context summary from recent conversation turns (for continuity)
  • An anonymous session identifier (randomly generated, not linked to you)
  • An emotional shift signal (a numeric delta value, not message content)

This data is processed in real time to generate a response and is not stored on our servers after the response is delivered. It is ephemeral.

Passive Listening Mode

When you activate passive listening mode (the Feel tab), the app accesses your device microphone to measure ambient audio energy levels. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored. Sound is analyzed locally in real time to determine volume and spectral characteristics only. No speech recognition or transcription occurs in passive mode.

Third-Party Services

Our services use Anthropic's Claude API to generate AI responses. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic does not train on API inputs per their data usage policy. We use Cloudflare for hosting and serverless compute. We use GitHub Pages for static site delivery.

Children

MemoryRX is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please use the service with a parent or guardian present. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and because we store no personal information on our servers, there is no child data to collect.

Data Deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you control deletion entirely:

  • In the app: Soul tab → "reset everything" deletes all local data
  • In your browser: Clear site data for heuremen.org
  • On your device: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

No request to us is needed because we don't have your data.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Material changes to data handling will be noted prominently.
