WHAT YOU ARE WATCHING

Each data point is generated by quantum vacuum fluctuations inside a photonic chip. The device measures genuine quantum randomness — not algorithmic pseudo-random numbers. Every value is fundamentally unpredictable, a direct readout of nature collapsing from superposition.

In Sim Mode, your browser's crypto.getRandomValues() provides cryptographic randomness as a stand-in. Same mathematics, different photons. For live QRNG, authenticate at orbit.quantum-dice.com.

The round faces of a quantum die are not cosmetic — they are the physical embodiment of the pre-collapse moment. A flat face implies a settled surface. A curved face holds all possibilities simultaneously, right up until the moment the world decides.
