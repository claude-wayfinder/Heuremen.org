What you're watching

This visualizer connects to Quantum Dice Ltd's photonic QRNG device — a chip that uses fundamental quantum randomness to produce numbers that are truly unpredictable. If their live server is unavailable (it requires certificate auth), the visualizer automatically falls back to your browser's own cryptographic random number generator (crypto.getRandomValues()), labelled as SIM mode. The physics is the same — only the source differs.

Purple (QRNG Value): The quantum random byte (0–255).

Teal (Raw Bits): The raw bit stream before post-processing — closest to raw quantum noise.

Red (Entropy): A measure of unpredictability per sample. Quantum entropy is the gold standard because it has no hidden pattern — ever.

The line never settles. That's not a bug. The die has no memory. Every roll is genuinely new.
