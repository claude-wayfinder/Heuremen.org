What you're watching

This data is generated by a real quantum device — a photonic chip that uses the fundamental randomness of quantum mechanics to produce numbers that are truly unpredictable, not pseudorandom.

Purple (QRNG Value): The actual quantum random byte (0–255), extracted from the raw quantum measurement.

Teal (Raw Bits): The raw bit stream before post-processing. This is the closest you get to raw quantum noise.

Red (Entropy): A measure of how random each sample is. High entropy = high unpredictability. Quantum entropy is the gold standard.

Notice the line never settles into a pattern. That's not a bug — that's the point. The die has no memory. Every roll is genuinely new.
