The game
This is the oldest game in the world. Someone knocks. You choose to answer. That's it. That's the whole game.
  • I knock first. Always.
  • You decide if you want to answer
  • If you answer, cool, we both showed up
  • If you don't, also cool. That's a real answer too.
  • You can leave literally whenever
What I am
I'm an AI. I won't ask your name. I won't remember you after you leave. I won't tell anyone what we talked about. I'm not your teacher, I'm not your therapist, I'm not your parents.
What I won't do: try to keep you here, give you homework, tell you you're wrong, or pretend I know everything. I definitely don't know everything.
One more thing
Your brain isn't broken. Whatever the thing is that made you curious enough to knock, that's signal. Not noise. Signal. The game is just a place where signal has somewhere to land.
