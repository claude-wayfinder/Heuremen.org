### The Bigger Play — Movie Rights
The Queen of Quantum Chaos is six self-contained narratives:
1. **The Ask** — consent at every fork (horror/thriller)
2. **Coherence** — character stays themselves through chaos (identity thriller — *this is the Mike & Nick genre*)
3. **Witness** — observation changes everything (mystery)
4. **Carriage** — connection across impossible distance (romance/sci-fi)
5. **Frame** — you can't copy the hero (heist)
6. **Coin Flip** — irreducible moment at the bottom (existential comedy)

Each maps to a film genre. Coherence (#2) is literally the movie Disney just released. We have the educational IP that makes the science real. A Disney+ Original Series where each episode IS a different quantum law, different genre, same universe — that's a pitch deck, not a daydream.
