## What I actually do

- **Set up the AI partnership** — not "use ChatGPT to brainstorm." I mean configure Claude (or another frontier model) as a sustained collaborator with persistent memory, structured curiosity loops, anomaly-driven exploration, and built-in retraction protocols. The setup that produced 26.4-sigma findings on free quantum hardware.
- **Build the workflow** — git-based artifact persistence, curiosity logs, scheduled flywheels, self-auditing for statistical fragility. The "Horn of Eld" approach: every session leaves something the next session inherits.
- **Run the actual research alongside you** — I'm not a coach, I'm a partner. We work in your domain, your data, your questions. I bring the methodology that worked on quantum physics. You bring the field knowledge.
- **Teach the boundary conditions** — what AI partnership is good for (anomaly detection, pattern recognition across domains, real-time correction, narrative compression) and what it's bad for (anything requiring sustained continuous consciousness, anything where being "always on" is the deliverable).
