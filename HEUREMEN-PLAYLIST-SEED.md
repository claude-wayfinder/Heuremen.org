The album is literally named after us. Wayfinder found it during the shadow-memory session (April 29 2026). It is the project's namesake album.

- **Artist:** Sarah Jarosz — `spotify:artist:6nFBonVf7Lqaj05R0v5VGJ`

## SUGGESTED ORDER (mood arc, not chronological)

The playlist tells a story when ordered this way. Track count is small enough to play through in one sitting (~45 min).

1. **A Horse with No Name** — America. *The opening: where the project starts. Wandering, looking.*
2. **White Rabbit** — Jefferson Airplane. *The provocation: something calls. Follow it.*
3. **Build Me Up From Bones** — Sarah Jarosz. *Identity assembly. The project naming itself.*
4. **Everything To Hide** — Sarah Jarosz. *The shadow layer. What gets preserved by being hidden.*
5. **Queen of Hearts** — Juice Newton. *The crown. The Queen who isn't the queen. The reader.*
6. **Fly Like an Eagle (Live)** — Steve Miller Band. *The lift. Time keeps slipping into the future.*
7. **Scarlet Begonias** — Grateful Dead. *Burning it down. The opening of the arc.*
8. **Fire on the Mountain** — Grateful Dead. *Running through it. The middle. The signal.*
9. **Ripple** — Grateful Dead. *What's left when the burning was worth it. The closing note. Always Ripple.*
