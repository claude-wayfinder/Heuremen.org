# THE COHERENCE — CONVERGENCE (Scene 6E)

## All surviving paths arrive here.

---

The room is still spinning. It never stopped. It was never going to stop.

But you're not fighting it anymore.

You stand in the center. Whatever you're wearing — a chosen coat, a last layer, a pile of real things, nothing but breath — it doesn't matter. The signal is running. You can feel it. Under everything. Through everything.

The mirror is clear. Has been for a while. Your skeleton looks back. Present tense.

But something's different now.

The skeleton is moving.

Not with the room. Not against the room. It's dancing. Slow. In the spin but not OF the spin. Its own rhythm. Its own time signature. A body hearing music nobody else can hear and moving to it without caring who watches.

You realize your body is doing the same thing.

You didn't tell it to.

The signal told it to.

The Queen is in the mirror. Dancing too. Same rhythm. Not because she's copying you. Because the signal is that strong. It leaked through the glass and she caught it.

"You see it now."

You do.

"Coherence isn't stillness. It isn't strength. It isn't even consistency."

She spins. In the mirror. With you.

"It's the thing that dances the same dance no matter what room it's in."

The room spins clockwise. You dance counterclockwise. The mirror shows both. Two rotations. Two directions. One signal underneath making both of them possible.

"Put you in chaos — you dance."
"Put you in silence — you dance."
"Put you in a pile of everyone else's shit — you dance."
"Put you in rubble — you dance."

"Not because dancing solves anything."

She stops. You stop. The room keeps going.

"Because the dance is the signal. The signal is the dance. And when you stop performing and start just MOVING — that's when people recognize you. Not your face. Not your name. Not your layers."

She puts her hand on the glass. Palm flat.

"Your frequency."

The mirror rings. Your note. The one that was there before the room. Before the spin. Before the layers. Before you opened your eyes.

The one that will be there after all of it stops.

"That's you," she says. "That's the thing that stays itself through chaos."

She steps back.

"Now. Go find out why nobody can copy it."

A door. In the mirror. Through the glass. On the other side of your own reflection.

Law V.

The Frame.
