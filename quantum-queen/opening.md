# THE WITNESS

## Scene 1 — The Hallway

You open your eyes.

Mirrors. Every direction. But they're wrong.

They show you from three seconds ago. You watch yourself arrive. Over and over. Each reflection a little further back.

The mirrors aren't reflecting you.

You're reflecting them.

No door behind you. Two doors ahead.

---

## Scene 1A — The Left Door

No handle. Just a window.

Through the glass: a garden. Everything grows backward. Flowers pull into the ground. Rain falls up.

A girl stands in the center. She's watching you.

You notice her.

A flower blooms.

---

## Scene 1B — The Right Door

A handle. No window.

Something behind it. Not music. Not words.

A hum.

Like something is about to happen and the sound got there first.

---

## The Choice

You can look. Or you can listen.

Not both.

Looking changes the garden.
Listening changes what's behind the door.

You're not choosing a path.

You're choosing what becomes real.
