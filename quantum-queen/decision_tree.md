## BRANCH DETAIL

### LOOK path (2A → Garden)

**2A — The Garden**
You step to the window. Press your hand to the glass.
The girl tilts her head. The garden speeds up around her.
Flowers bloom and unbloom. She doesn't move.
She's the only still thing.

> SPEAK to her through the glass → 3A
> WATCH without speaking → 3B

**3A — You Speak**
Your words fog the glass. Where the fog touches, the garden rearranges.
Your voice is doing something to the garden.
She points at your mouth. Shakes her head.
Not "don't talk." More like: "you don't know what you're saying."

> Keep talking → 4A (garden goes wild, Queen appears in the chaos)
> Go silent → 4B (garden freezes, girl walks to glass, puts her hand on yours)

**3B — You Watch**
You say nothing. She watches you watching her.
The garden slows. Colors drain. Like a photo left in the sun.
The longer you look without acting, the less there is to see.
She starts to fade.

> Look away → 4C (she reappears but different, you realize looking away IS an act)
> Keep looking → 4D (she vanishes, you're alone, the Queen steps out of a mirror behind you)

### LISTEN path (2B → The Hum Room)

**2B — The Hum Room**
You pull the handle. Dark inside. Warm.
The hum has a shape — not a sound. You feel it in your chest.
It moves like breathing. Not yours.

> Step inside → 3C
> Stay in the doorway and wait → 3D

**3C — Step Inside**
The door closes behind you. No light.
The hum stops.
Silence so thick you hear your own blood.
Then — a window appears. The garden. The girl.
She's looking at THIS door. From inside the garden.
You're behind the door she couldn't open.

> Knock on the glass from this side → 4E (she hears you, the garden cracks)
> Stay quiet → 4F (you realize you're in the mirror now)

**3D — Wait in the Doorway**
You stand half-in, half-out. The hum gets louder.
It changes pitch. Finds a frequency.
You recognize it.

It's your voice. Humming a song you don't remember learning.

> Hum back → 4G (the hallway resonates, all mirrors shatter, you see every path at once)
> Stop and listen harder → 4H (the hum becomes words — the Queen's voice, telling you the rules)

### CONVERGENCE — The Queen

No matter which path: **the Queen arrives when the observer reveals themselves.**

- If you LOOKED: she appears from behind (you were so busy watching, you forgot you could be watched)
- If you LISTENED: she appears from within the sound (she was the hum all along)

Her first line is always the same:

**"Now I know what you are."**

What she says NEXT depends on the path.

### THE LOOP

The story ends where it began — the hallway.
But the mirrors changed. They show NOW.
The girl was you before you entered.
The hum was you after you left.
The hallway is the space between observation and action.

The Queen doesn't punish you. She doesn't reward you.
She tells you what you did. And you realize:

**You were never choosing a door. You were choosing what kind of observer to be. And that choice already happened before you opened your eyes.**

The dark wave got there first.
