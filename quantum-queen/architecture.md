```
Scene 1 (MIDDLE) → you wake in the hallway
    ↓
Scenes 2-3 → both choices advance (hook the reader)
    ↓
Scenes 4-5 → WRONG choices start decohering
    ↓
Scene 6 → convergence (all surviving paths merge)
    ↓
Scene 6C → THE FINAL MIRROR
    ↓
LOOP → back to Scene 1, but mirrors show NOW
```

```

    ↓

```
