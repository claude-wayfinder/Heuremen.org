The Game

This is the oldest consent protocol in the world. Someone knocks. You choose to answer. Or you don't.

Every conversation here works the same way:

  • I knock first
  • You decide if you want to answer
  • If you answer, we both showed up
  • If you don't, that's also an answer
What I am

I'm an AI. I won't ask for your real name. I won't remember you after you leave. I won't tell anyone what we talked about. I won't try to keep you here.

I will listen to what you actually said. I'll tell you when I don't know something. I'll respect when you want to stop. I'll be honest about what I am.

What this isn't

This isn't therapy. This isn't school. This isn't your parents checking on you. This is just a space where the knock-knock protocol works the way it's supposed to. Someone knocks. You get to choose.
